Ingredients for Chicken Biriyani:


1. Basmati Rice 500 gms.
2. 4 Chicken leg piece
3. Preparation for chicken marination:
-Ginger, garlic, tomato, green chili, onion (Grind it to make a thick paste
-plain yogurt 1/2 cup
4. Wholesome spices for cooking rice:
-Cardamom, cinnamon, cloves, star anise, black cardamom , nutmeg, mace , black cumin (shah jeera),
white pepper, bay leaves.
5. Biriyani masala (readymade)
6. Rose water
7. Kewra water
8. Mitha Ittar
9. Saffron milk
10. Sugar
11. Oil
12. Ghee
13. Salt (as per taste)
14. Sliced Onion for preparation of making fried onion (Beresta)
15. Potato (Optional)

Preparation:


• Wash Basmati rice and soak it for 30 mins
• Clean the chicken and marinate with yogurt and grinded paste for 30 mins
• Put water into the pot for cooking rice. Once the water started boiling add whole spices(Cardamom,
cinnamon, cloves,cloves, star anise, black cardamom , nutmeg, mace , black cumin (shah jeera),
white pepper, bay leaves). Let the whole
spices boil into the water for 10 mins. Now after 10 mins of boiling remove the whole spices then
add 1 teaspoon oil,
2 teaspoon salt and 1 teaspoon sugar and add soaked rice for cooking around 80% .
• Put some oil & ghee into the kadai and once oil heated add onion to fry until golden brown(Prepare Beresta)
• Add some oil into the kadai and let it heat then add marinated chicken into the kadai. Let it cook for
15 min in
a low heat. Then add fried onion (Beresta) and ½ teaspoon garam masala, 1 teaspoon biriyani masala and
add salt as per taste.
• Now its time for final step for biriyani layering. First sprinkle some biriyani masala into the cooked
rice and mix it then follow
the layering step as per showing and put the vessel in a low flame dum for about 15 mins
• After 15 mins take the vessel out the flame and now its ready to serve.
